Main crew members

Monkey D. Luffy

Luffy's the crews captain and one of the most wanted pirates in the Grand Line. Currently holds the positon of a Yonko, Emperor of the Sea with a B3,000,000,000 bounty. He will become the Pirate King.

Roronoa Zoro

Roronoa Zoro one of the main fighters of the crew with a unwavering loyalty to the captain, Luffy. He aspires to become the greatest swordsman and is currently the only known user of the Three Sword Style.

Nami

"Cat Burglar" Nami, the navigator for the crew and 2nd to join. Previously affiliated with the Arlong Pirates but has now joined Luffy on his journey to become Pirate King.

Ussop

"God" Ussop, the sniper of the crew and 3rd to join. He is the son of Yassop, member of Red Hair Pirates. Despite his usual cowardice, Ussop dreams of being a brave warrior of the sea like his father.
